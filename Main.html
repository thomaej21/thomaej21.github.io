At Space-Cat, our commitment to innovation has led us to new ways of bridging the gap between basic science research, medical education, and quality of patient care. We also now offer a groundbreaking accelerated program so students can get their MD dgree in 3 years instead of 4. Students who are accepted into this honoaray program will recive a fliltution scholorship. This program limits the flexibility of a students ability to customize their degree since they will be completing their degree in a faster time schedile than normal. Summber break will be only 3 weeks long to give students time to rest before starting to work again.
